What is PaceMe

The idea arose when Tim contracted COVID. He became very ill and developed long COVID as a result. The symptoms he has experienced since then have a major impact on his daily life. Because there are still few effective medical treatments, there is no medication that truly provides relief. Tim wants to help others going through the same thing—as well as people with illnesses that have similar symptoms—so they can gradually regain control of their day, step by step. That is why he started looking for a digital solution that makes managing energy practical, supportive, and human.

From that, PaceMe was born: an app that helps people regain control of their daily lives.
